Why is Deepfake Detection Necessary?
Deepfake technology poses a significant threat in the digital world. Fake videos and images can lead to misinformation, damage individuals' reputations, and create social unrest. Therefore, deepfake detection plays a critical role in distinguishing real content from fake ones. It is crucial for maintaining the reliability of information sources and protecting individuals' digital rights.

Manipulating People During Wartime

Deepfake technology can be used during wartime to create fake videos or audio recordings, manipulating people's beliefs and emotions. Detecting deepfakes can help prevent such manipulation and protect the integrity of information.

Dirty Political Agendas

Politicians may use deepfake technology to create misleading content, advancing their political agendas through unethical means. Detecting deepfakes can help uncover these deceptive practices and maintain the integrity of political discourse.

Deceiving the Public

Deepfakes can be used to spread false information, deceiving the public by making it difficult to distinguish between real and fake content. Effective deepfake detection can help expose these falsehoods and ensure that the public receives accurate information.
